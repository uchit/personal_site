Long · 250 words~250 wds

Uchit Vyas is Core Modernisation, Platform Engineering & AI Lead at Accenture ANZ, based in Melbourne. He has spent 15+ years helping Tier-1 banks, telcos and government departments move from ambitious digital strategy to live, audited production — across enterprise architecture, cloud transformation, DevSecOps, SRE, data modernisation, and most recently applied GenAI inside regulated environments.

His career spans Accenture ANZ (since 2022), KPMG Singapore as Associate Director in CIO Advisory (2021–22), Accenture ASEAN as Principal Technology Architect (2017–21), Oracle as Senior Technology Architect, plus earlier roles at REAN Cloud, Infosys, Attune Infocom and TCS.

He is the author of five books on cloud architecture, DevSecOps and integration patterns, published by Apress and Packt. He holds US patent US11334348B2 (“Specs Monster — an intelligent design collaboration tool”). He has been recognised on the Top 50 DevSecOps Influencers list globally, and received internal Bunsen Burner and Delivery Heroes awards at Accenture. He has delivered 30+ talks and keynotes at industry conferences across ASEAN, Europe and the United States, including HashiConf, APIDays, Cloud Expo Asia, and TopConf.

He is the author of The 4-Discipline Stack — the practitioner framework for how enterprise architecture, platform engineering & DevSecOps, data modernisation and applied GenAI compound through a shared operating substrate — and publishes the annual State of Enterprise Tech in Regulated Industries report at hellouchit.com.
