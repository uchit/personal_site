Build or buy an AI gateway.

An AI gateway sits between your applications and LLM providers — handling routing, prompt-versioning, evals, guardrails, observability, audit, cost attribution. Build is rarely the answer; this tree walks the conditions where buy / build / skip-entirely actually wins.

  3. Do you have, or are you funding, a GenAI platform team of 3+ engineers?
