What he does.

Uchit Vyas leads technology-transformation engagements for regulated-industry clients (banks, government agencies, healthcare, critical infrastructure operators) at Accenture, ANZ. His work spans the four disciplines he calls the 4-Discipline Stack:

  • Enterprise architecture — SAFe, BIAN, multi-cloud reference models, capability modelling, decision capture (ADRs).
  • Platform engineering & DevSecOps — paved paths, policy-as-code, security in CI/CD, internal developer platforms, SLSA-aligned supply-chain integrity.
  • Data modernisation — trusted, lineage-aware, analytics-ready data platforms; BCBS 239-grade data governance.
  • Applied GenAI — architecture, evals, guardrails, governance for regulated environments; NIST AI RMF, ISO 42001, EU AI Act readiness.
